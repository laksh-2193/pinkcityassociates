About Us

PinkCity Associates

Founded in 2020, PinkCity Associates is a Jaipur based firm providing asset management services. Our focus is Indian listed equities and real estate. We cater to corporates, institutions, family offices, and individuals from India and globally. The investment management team at PinkCity Associates brings more than 20 years of cumulative experience in investment management.

At PinkCity, we believe investing does not have to be complicated and we keep it that way. We screen investment opportunities based on strict parameters of:

Growth

To look for accelerating current annual & quarterly earnings.

Quality

To identify leading stocks from the leading industries.

Competitive Advantage

To identify companies having product/ service leadership.

Philosophy

At PinkCity, our philosophy is inspired by William O Neil's CANSLIM style of investment management to primarily invest in leading, high quality, high growth businesses that are run by honest and capable management teams.
